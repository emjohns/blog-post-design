Top 5 Restaurants in Charleston, SC

...according to Emma

In a city known for its food scene, these are my five favorite places to eat. Check them out and let me know what you think!


  • Moe's Crosstown Tavern

    If you're craving a burger in downtown Charleston, Moe's Tavern is the place to go. This dive bar serves all the staples you could want for a price and taste that cannot be beat. There are new specials every day of the week and Tuesdays are a must-visit with half-priced burgers from 7-10pm.

  • Hominy Grill

    This is one of the quintessential Southern restaurants in a city of Southern restaurants. If you're looking for shrimp and grits, this may be the place to go.

  • The Taco Spot and Apartment A

    Looking for cheap and flavorful bites with a creative and affordable cocktail menu? Look no further than the Taco Spot on Cannon and Ashley. They expanded into Apartment A and have a great happy hour without the crowds. Just a tip, always go with the crispy, corn tortillas!

  • Xiao Bao Biscuit

    This is not your typical Asian fusion. This restaurant is one of the very best of a city of bests. The food is inventive and ever-changing while being accessible and maintaining a good price-point. Order multiple plates and make sure everyone in your party gets a taste of everything. Xiao Bao is a truly excellent experience.

  • My Kitchen

    You cannot get the variety, nutrition, or price at any restaurant like you can in my own kitchen. While a hefty load of vegetables is guaranteed, the protein and ethnic influence are constantly changing.
